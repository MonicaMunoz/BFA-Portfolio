Artist Statement

 

I am an artist who cares about giving my audience art that is entertaining and appealing. My work is based on making art conceptual and educational. I believe that art can be expressed in many ways as something pleasing to view and didactic in a simultaneous manner. To me, art has always been of great importance. At a truly young age, I recall spending most of my spare time drawing and coloring things that related to nature and people. I knew then that I wanted to pursue an artistic career.
My artworks vary in mediums, but ultimately most center on digital art. I enjoy creating art in the traditional way such as painting and also programming imageries. Interestingly enough, minoring in Computer Science is a great aspect for me to construct art I absolutely adore. My ambition to be a part of the BFA program is to learn more about technology based art and concepts so that I can apply to them to my future employment. I am a person that appreciates working the mind by facing difficult challenges. Digital media art has definitely taught me to view art an excitedly new way.
