Chained to our bodies

I created this artwork for one of my classes in the topic of Micro/Macro. Its is based on is on the statistics on Lymphoma Cancer. According to research,It is one of the most common cancers in the US.
I decided to make this project on this topic because I know how devastating and silent it can be in a personal level. A very close relative of mine unfortunately experienced it.
Cancer is essentially the distortion of DNA. At a Micro level, it affects the individual (inside the DNA strand)(small scale). At the Macro level, it affects certain people as a whole (large scale).
To make this piece, I made an illustration of it on photoshop and animated it through Pixaloop. Here, are two hands chained by a DNA strand that is mutated/broken. The image itself is abstracted has red colors around the hands which symbolize heat as a real patient would feel from the symptoms(fever and night sweats). At the bottom, there are firework like effects that symbolize the sudden impact someone who has the condition might feel when diagnosed. This artwork demonstrates how we are chained to our body/DNA and how our body gets attacked(chaos in the body) at random no matter how healthy we live our lifestyles. In the end it all depends on our body and how we react to those factors.
